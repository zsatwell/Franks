What's our story?

The story of Frank's Flame Grill started all the way back in 1990, when Frank Fire got a job at a local burger resturant in his hometown of Utopia, Texas as a dishwasher. Over the years he moved up the positions, until he became the manager. His time here is what developed his love of cooking delicious burgers, with fresh ingredients. In 2012 he moved to Waco, Texas to start his own food truck selling, afordable, delicious, and high quality burgers, and Frank's Flame Grill was born.
